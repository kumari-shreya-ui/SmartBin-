About Ecogenesis

Ecogenesis is a project dedicated to solving modern waste management challenges through technology. Our smart bin prototype uses sensors and intelligent sorting mechanisms to automatically segregate waste into organic, recyclable, and hazardous materials. By providing real-time data, we aim to optimize collection routes, promote recycling, and create a more sustainable future for our communities.

Our future plans include a dedicated mobile app that will allow municipal corporations and users to track the current fill status of the bins in real-time, receive alerts when bins are full, and view detailed analytics on waste collection.
